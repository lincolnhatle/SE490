# Asterisk: Game Guide

Asterisk is a fast-paced, web-based survival and avoidance game. You navigate a constantly moving path, dodging obstacles and surviving as long as possible. The game features both a single-player progression mode and a two-player competitive survival mode.

## 🎯 The Objective

The primary goal of Asterisk is to **reach the right side of the screen** without crashing into anything. When you do, you'll instantly progress to the next level, which will be more difficult.

**You must avoid crashing into:**
1. **White Borders:** The top, bottom, and right edges of the canvas are solid walls (except for a gap on the right).
2. **Asterisks (`*`):** White asterisks are randomly scattered across the play area. Hitting one means game over! The number of asterisks increases with each level.
3. **Player Trails:** In a 2-player game, players leave behind a colored trail. Crashing into either player's trail acts as a solid obstacle.

## 🕹️ Controls

Your player character moves forward (to the right) automatically. You only control your vertical movement (up and down). 

By default, your character naturally falls **downwards**. Pressing your assigned key will propel your character **upwards**.

- **Player 1:** 
  - **Hold the `Enter` key** to move UP.
  - **Release the `Enter` key** to move DOWN.
  
- **Player 2:** 
  - **Hold the `Shift` key** to move UP.
  - **Release the `Shift` key** to move DOWN.

> [!TIP]
> Navigating effectively requires rhythmic tapping of your key to "hover" in place or timing your presses to squeeze between the randomized asterisks!

## 🎮 Game Modes

### 1 Player Mode
- **Goal:** Reach the highest level possible. 
- You start at the middle of the screen (vertically).
- Each level you clear, the game spawns more asterisks (`Level Number × 3`).
- **High Scores:** The game tracks and saves the highest level reached along with the player's name locally. If you beat the local high score, you'll be prompted to enter your name!

### 2 Player Mode
- **Goal:** Outlast your opponent! 
- Both players are on the screen simultaneously. Player 1 starts near the top, and Player 2 starts near the bottom.
- If a player crashes into an obstacle, they lose, and the surviving player wins the round.
- If both crash at the exact same time, the round ends in a draw.

## 🌈 Visuals and Levels
- Player 1 is represented by a trail that changes color every time a new level is reached.
- Player 2 is always represented by a light green trail.
- Every map has an entirely unique layout of asterisks, generated randomly at the start of the level.
- Keep progressing to see your trail color cycle through yellow, cyan, magenta, orange, pink, deepskyblue, and lime!
